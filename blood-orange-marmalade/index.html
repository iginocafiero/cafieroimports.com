Marmellata di Arancia Rossa

Made from Sicilian blood oranges at peak season — when the color runs deepest and the flavor hits hardest. Bittersweet, complex, and unlike any marmalade you have had before.

  • Origin Siracusa, Sicily
  • Fruit Tarocco & Moro blood oranges
  • Season January – March harvest
  • Process Small-batch, copper kettle

The blood oranges of southeastern Sicily are unlike anything grown elsewhere. The dramatic temperature swings between Etna’s cool nights and the Mediterranean sun produce a fruit with deep crimson flesh, intense sweetness, and a bitter edge that makes extraordinary marmalade.
