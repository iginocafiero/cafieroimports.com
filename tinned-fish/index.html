Caught in season off the Sicilian coast and packed in local olive oil. The tinned fish tradition here goes back centuries. This is not pantry tuna ☤ this is something you put on a plate and pour a glass of wine for.

CENTURIES
AT SEA.

Sicily has been at the center of Mediterranean tuna fishing since the Phoenicians. The traditional mattanza — a centuries-old method of catching bluefin tuna — defined entire coastal communities here. That deep respect for the sea and its rhythms still runs through every producer we work with.

Our tuna is line-caught during the spring and summer season, hand-packed into tins, and covered in Sicilian extra virgin olive oil. No water-packing, no soy oil, no industrial shortcuts. Just exceptional fish preserved the way it has been done here for generations.

  • OriginSicily, Italy — Mediterranean coast
  • SpeciesYellowfin tuna (Thunnus albacares)
  • MethodLine-caught, hand-packed
  • IngredientsTuna, Sicilian extra virgin olive oil, sea salt
  • Size200g tin
