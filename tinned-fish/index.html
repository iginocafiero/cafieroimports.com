THE MATTANZA
TRADITION

Sicily has been at the center of Mediterranean tuna fishing since the Phoenicians. The traditional mattanza — a centuries-old method of catching bluefin tuna — defined entire coastal communities here. That deep respect for the sea and its rhythms still runs through every producer we work with. Our tuna is line-caught during the spring and summer season, hand-packed into tins, and covered in Sicilian extra virgin olive oil. No water-packing, no soy oil, no industrial shortcuts. Just exceptional fish preserved the way it has been done here for generations.
